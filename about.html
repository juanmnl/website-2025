About Me

My Journey into Product Design

Hi, I'm Juan Manuel, a Product Designer with 20+ years of experience creating meaningful digital experiences. My path started two decades ago with Flash animations and Dreamweaver layouts, where I discovered how powerful thoughtful design can be in shaping people's interactions with technology. I focus on transforming complex challenges into intuitive, elegant solutions that truly serve user needs.

I thrive in cross-functional teams, partnering with engineers, product managers, and stakeholders to take concepts from initial spark to successful launch. My approach centers on user research, rapid prototyping, and embracing continuous improvement throughout the design process.

Outside of Design

When I'm not doing research or designing interfaces, you can find me behind a camera, lost in a good book, or putting thoughts to paper. These pursuits continually sharpen my eye for detail, strengthen my storytelling abilities, and fuel the creative thinking that flows into my design work.

I'm always eager to connect with fellow designers, explore new opportunities, or discuss where product design is heading next. Feel free to reach out!
